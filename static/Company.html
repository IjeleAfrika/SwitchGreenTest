Company

Who We Are

SwitchGreen Energy Ltd. is a Nigerian startup from Enugu. We focus on low-voltage switchboard and control panel designs for commercial and industrial clients.

We work exclusively with the Scandinavian Logstrup brand, custom-building switchboards following the best engineering standards.

We are also open to other interesting energy-related projects, including green generation and storage solutions.

OUR MISSION

To be the best and leading company in the manufacture and sale of switches, switchgear, and green energy solutions in Africa.

OUR VISION

To be the reference, go-to company for switchgear and green energy solutions in Nigeria.

CORE VALUES

With our wealth of experience, we have a dynamic approach to solving every switching problem, through:

  1. Integrity
  2. Honesty
  3. Professional competence
  4. Technology and Innovations
OUR PEOPLE

We are a pooled resource company that relies on the expertise of a wide range of professionals to design and implement projects as they come. We will continue developing into an exemplary team with the technical expertise for efficiently handling the most complex challenges.
